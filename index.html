About Bondhu Poribar

  • Our mission is to empower the youth of Bangladesh through volunteerism, fostering self-resilience for a meaningful future.
  • Bondhu Proibar was founded in 2017 by a group of passionate frined who wanted to make a positive impact on their communities.
  • Our values include a commitment to diversity, equality, and inclusion, as well as a focus on community-drivensolutions and sustainable development.
  • We believe that volunteerism is a powerful tool for creating positive cahnge in Bangladesh and we are dedicated to providing opportunities for individuals to get involved and make a difference
